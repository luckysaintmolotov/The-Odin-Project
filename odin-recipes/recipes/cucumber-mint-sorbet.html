### Cucumber and Mint Sorbet **Description:** Cool off with this refreshing Cucumber and Mint Sorbet, a perfect treat for hot summer days! This light and invigorating sorbet combines the crispness of cucumbers with the aromatic freshness of mint, creating a delightful balance of flavors. The natural sweetness from sugar and a hint of lime juice elevate the taste, making it a wonderfully refreshing dessert or palate cleanser. Easy to make and incredibly satisfying, this sorbet is not only a delicious way to beat the heat but also a healthy option that’s sure to impress your guests. Serve it in bowls or cones for a delightful summer indulgence that’s both light and revitalizing! ### Ingredients: - 2 large cucumbers, peeled and chopped - 1/2 cup fresh mint leaves - 1/2 cup sugar (or to taste) - 1 cup water - Juice of 1 lime ### Instructions: 1. In a blender, combine the chopped cucumbers, mint leaves, sugar, water, and lime juice. Blend until smooth. 2. Strain the mixture through a fine mesh sieve to remove any pulp, if desired. 3. Pour the liquid into a shallow dish and freeze for about 2 hours. 4. After 2 hours, use a fork to scrape the mixture, breaking up any ice crystals. Return to the freezer. 5. Repeat the scraping process every 30 minutes until the sorbet is fully frozen and fluffy. 6. Serve in bowls or cones for a refreshing summer treat!
