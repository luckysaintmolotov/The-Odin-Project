Ingredients: 2 ripe mangoes, diced 1 small red onion, finely chopped 1-2 jalape単o peppers, seeded and minced (adjust based on your spice preference) 1 red bell pepper, diced 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped Juice of 1 lime Salt to taste Optional: 1 avocado, diced (for creaminess) Instructions: Prepare the Ingredients: Start by dicing the mangoes, red onion, red bell pepper, and jalape単os. If using, dice the avocado as well. Mix the Salsa: In a large bowl, combine the diced mangoes, red onion, jalape単os, red bell pepper, and cilantro. Add Lime Juice: Squeeze the lime juice over the mixture and gently toss to combine. This will add flavor and help prevent the mango from browning. Season: Add salt to taste. If you like it spicier, you can add more jalape単o or even a pinch of cayenne pepper. Optional Avocado: If using avocado, gently fold it in last to avoid mashing it. Chill and Serve: Let the salsa sit for about 15-30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld. Serve with tortilla chips, grilled chicken, fish, or as a topping for tacos.
